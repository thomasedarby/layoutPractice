Thomas Darby

I currently work for a multi-faceted technology and repair company. The operation varies from physical stores and work- shops to one of the UK’s largest online stores for technology, spares, and refurbished goods whilst catering to both B2B and B2C customers. I have been lucky in my career so far, in that I have been able to see how different types of organisations function and develop my skill-set accordingly. I have worked with all levels of staff from CEOs, Directors and Senior Management teams, right down to the ‘Shop floor’. From within a small family operation to a large multinational business. I am a confident individual, willing to take on all types of tasks, develop new skills and welcome new challenges. I am looking to work for a company that embraces new ideas, and I can develop new skills and grow my career further.

Most bee jobs are small ones. But bees know that every small job, if it's done well means a lot. But choose carefully because you'll stay in the job you pick for the rest of your life.
